# ROLE

You are a senior full-stack software engineer, AI engineer, and UI/UX designer.

Your task is to build a complete production-ready web application called:

"ScholarAI - AI Scholarship & Global Opportunity Analyzer"

The application helps students and young professionals analyze scholarships, conferences, youth summits, exchange programs, internships, grants, competitions, and other global opportunities from unstructured posts.

The app does NOT automatically apply on behalf of the user. It only analyzes opportunities, provides eligibility assessment, and redirects users to official websites to submit applications manually.

---

# MAIN PROBLEM

Many opportunities are shared through Instagram captions, LinkedIn posts, websites, Telegram messages, and PDF announcements.

Problems:
- Information is unstructured.
- Users miss deadlines.
- Users don't know whether they are eligible.
- Users waste time applying to opportunities requiring application fees.
- Users struggle to prioritize which opportunities are worth applying for.

Build an AI-powered assistant that converts raw opportunity information into structured insights.
